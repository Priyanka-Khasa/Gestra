Best Results

How to use Gestra well

Lighting

Front lighting keeps fingers readable. Backlighting destroys detection accuracy.

Framing

Stay inside the preview zone. Edge drift breaks hold stability immediately.

Stability

Pause and hold before expecting action. Gestra rewards deliberate intent.

Tuning

Adjust confidence, hold timing, smoothing, and deadzone until it feels natural.
